To conclude my opinion, Digital marketing is a combination of marketing and psychological elements that merge together to influence consumers behaviour and thought process. This can only be achieved by having a clear understanding of how people think and how these thoughts affect how they act. Psychology has become a major part of digital advertising. Online marketers try to discover all the unseen factors of human nature, which might be helpful in attracting the attention of your customer base.

Digital marketing is an affordable and effective platform for businesses to promote their products and services. When you understand the basic needs, biases, and fears of your customers, you can start to sculpt your marketing strategies around them. However its important that your aim is never to exploit your customers, but to use psychological techniques to create a helpful and relevant experience for both you and your customers.

Over half of the world's population are online, It is the modern day platform for where the online buying process starts. It is also where consumers prefer you to engage with them. Therefore it is critical for businesses to adapt to a new advertising approach and move over to the world of Digital Marketing.
